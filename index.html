ABOUT US

Empowering Australian Businesses with Expert Virtual Administrative Support

Attainment Office Administrative Services (AOAS) is a dynamic startup founded in 2025, dedicated to providing exceptional virtual administrative services to Australian businesses. We specialize in partnering with NDIS healthcare institutions and various Australian industries to deliver comprehensive administrative solutions.

Our team of skilled Virtual Assistants is trained to handle a wide range of administrative tasks including task and calendar management, social media management, email management, rostering, worker monitoring reports, and more. We understand the unique requirements of Australian businesses and ensure our services align with local industry standards and regulations.

At AOAS, we are committed to delivering cost-effective and high-quality support that enables our clients to streamline operations, boost productivity, and achieve sustainable growth. Our values of Accountability, Operational Resilience, Adaptability, and Service-Driven approach guide everything we do.
